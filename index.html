AIMS:

To help us to:

- Identify the fruit of the Spirit

- Explain the marks of the Spirit filled life

- Aspire to live the Spirit filled life.

INTRODUCTION

The Spirit filled life is not a life without boundaries. The boundaries are set by the Holy Spirit. The exhibition of carnality in the Church today is increasing with the consequence of creating identity crisis in the body of Christ. This study is meant to lead us to discover the unique lifestyle of the Spirit filled life which is the solution to carnality and worldliness in the Church.
