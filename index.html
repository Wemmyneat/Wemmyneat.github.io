AIM:

To help believers understand the importance of Holiness.

To equip believers in Holiness in their journey to eternal life.

Building consciousness that Holiness is not natural.

INTRODUCTION

The way of holiness is a profound and foundational concept found throughout the Bible, encompassing God's call for His people to pursue a life of moral and spiritual purity. In various passages, both in the Old and New Testaments, we encounter expressions that illuminate the significance of this path. Isaiah 35:8

, speaks of a designated highway, symbolizing the clear and distinct path that leads believers toward holiness. It signifies a departure from impurity, sin, and unrighteousness, ensuring that only those who genuinely seek after God and embrace His ways can tread upon it.
However, the way of holiness is not a mere suggestion in scripture, but a foundational principle that is crucial for understanding God's desired relationship with His people. It highlights the continuous call for believers to separate themselves from the ways of the world, to pursue righteousness, and to live by God's divine nature.

Conclusion

The concept of holiness holds immense importance in the lives of believers, as it directly influences their ability to obtain eternal life. Holiness is not merely an external manifestation of religious practices, but rather a transformational journey that encompasses all aspects of one's being: mind, body, and spirit. The pursuit of holiness is rooted in the understanding that God, who is inherently holy, calls individuals to live a life set apart from the ways of the world. It entails cultivating a deep reverence and awe for God, recognizing His supreme authority, and aligning one's thoughts, actions, and desires with His divine will.
