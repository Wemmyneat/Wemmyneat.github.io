INTRODUCTION

The knowledge of God is one major and beautiful quality every Christian must desire. In knowing the Lord, we get to understand His ways. This makes following Him and His instructions easy and less cumbersome. It also reduces the mistakes that may even finally make the best of saints loose out his blessing in the end. God's demand that we know Him then, before doing exploit becomes an urgent decree every true believer must desire to obey. This study will help us understand why.
