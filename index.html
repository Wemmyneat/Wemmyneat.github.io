INTRODUCTION

These days are filled with lot of activities both spiritually and physically. The manifestations of the sons of God are becoming real as we experience daily, miracles in our midst and in the church of God. However, as in the olden days, the lying wonders of pharaoh's magicians and the activities of the sons` of Sceva have equally multiplied. These magic which may not only have brought ills and damages have also brought confusion into the household of faith. The end result being that only those who continue in the Spirit, and have been enabled to differentiate the real and fake will be spared the embarrassment. In this study we shall consider the issues of doing exploit and the spirit behind them.
