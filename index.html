Conclusion :

The pursuit of holiness, is a major part of the Christian life. It is important to know that every true believer of christ has been called to live a life that shine like stars in a crooked world and to pursue transformation towards divine perfection. However, it is important to know holiness is not possible by our works but it is by our walk with God. When we come to this consciousness, we'll no longer have to struggle to be holy because now we know that holy living is possible when we continually abide and walk with God.
