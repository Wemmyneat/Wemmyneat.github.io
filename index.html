INTRODUCTION

The foundation of every true believer is the call of God by grace and endowment with power according to the promises of God. However, for sustenance and continuity, the individual believer must continue in humility to depend on God for wisdom and strength not only to be alive in him and grow as a Christian, but also to do exploit. This is the dividing line between the standing Christians and those who may not make it in the end. In this study we will see how and why.
