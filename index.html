AIMS:

To help us understand the path in which we need to follow for transformation.


INTRODUCTION:

Our transformation into the life of God is not merely a perceptible change outwardly in behavior. After a person is saved, he might think that he was a mess as a non-Christian, but now that he is going to church and is also carrying a Bible, he should change his behavior. Transformation is not merely a noticeable change outwardly in behavior; rather, it is a metabolic transformation inwardly in essence. In this lesson we shall learn more deeply about transformation.

SUMMARY:

We were all created in the Image of God, something that no other species of plant or animal can claim. Because of this, we are offered a share in God’s divine essence, his goodness, his power, his majesty. We were created to walk with God and it is only through the transforming power of the Holy Spirit, given to us by the total sacrifice of Jesus Christ, that we can hope to live the life we were intended to live. God is offering you this life today, all you have to do is submit to his Spirit and it will be yours. Will you take that step? Will you completely submit to God?.
