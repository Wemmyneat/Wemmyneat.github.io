4. From the passage in (3) above, discuss the benefits we have if we keep to the demands

PERSONAL APPLICATION

We must obey His commands to keep on the Way. This can only be possible by diligently studying the word of God and remain unspotted.

CONCLUSION

We can't live a holy life without the help and the backing of the Holy Spirit, we should carry the image of God and diligently obey the Lord. God’s word places demands on believers. If such demands are met, believers will be tremendously blessed.
