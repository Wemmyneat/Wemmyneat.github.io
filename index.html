Aim:

To enable us:

- know the meaning of Quiet Time.

- reflect on its importance.

- examine the lifestyle of those who observed a quiet time in the bible.

INTRODUCTION

We must come to understand that the place of quiet time is very mandatory for our growth as Christians especially,a newly saved believer who,on receiving God's grace of salvation discovers that there is need for developing a regular(day-to-day) communion time with God; in the place of prayer and the study of the word of God. That,it might ensure sustainability in spiritual growth in all forms of godliness.In this study,we shall take a look at the meaning of Quiet Time, some people who observed their quiet time and its benefits with scriptural references.
