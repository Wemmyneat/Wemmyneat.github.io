AIMS:

To help us

- Understand what it means to pray in the Spirit

- Know the importance of praying in the Spirit.

INTRODUCTION

The importance of prayer cannot be overemphasized in the life of every believer. However, the mode and practice of prayer among some professing Christians today fall below expectation. It is not healthy to the faith they profess; therefore, there is need to have proper understanding of what it means to pray in the Spirit and to know how to be led by the Holy Spirit when we pray.
