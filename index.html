CONCLUSION

The outcome of your life is tied to your thought-life. An old poem rightly states: “Sow a thought...reap a destiny.” The greatest battle of life is fought, won or lost at the thought realm. The sure way to the victorious life is to keep our minds stayed on the Lord. It demands conscious decision and effort. The Lord gives grace!
